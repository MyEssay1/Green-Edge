About Green Edge

We're a locally owned landscaping company built on hard work, reliability, and genuine care for the properties we maintain.

Who We Are

Rooted in Wisconsin,
Built on Trust

Green Edge Landscaping was founded with a simple idea: provide dependable, quality landscaping services to homeowners and businesses in southeastern Wisconsin — without the hassle of dealing with a faceless company.

When you hire Green Edge, you get Nathan and Alec — not a crew you've never met. We take the time to understand your property, communicate clearly about what it needs, and show up when we say we will.

We serve Washington and Waukesha County with the kind of attention to detail that makes a real difference. From mowing and mulching to tree pruning and weed control, we treat every job — big or small — with the same level of care.
