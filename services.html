Our Services

From weekly maintenance to one-time projects, Green Edge delivers quality landscaping throughout Washington and Waukesha County.

Groundskeeping Photo

Service 01

Groundskeeping

A well-maintained lawn doesn't happen by accident. Our groundskeeping service keeps your property looking polished year-round — whether you're a homeowner who wants a tidy yard or a business that needs reliable curb appeal.

We work with a consistent schedule so your lawn always looks its best, and we handle the details so you don't have to think about it.

  • Lawn mowing and edging
  • Seasonal cleanup (spring & fall)
  • Bed edging and border maintenance
  • Debris and clipping removal
  • Walkway and driveway clearing
🪨

Mulching Photo

Service 02

Mulching

Fresh mulch does more than look great — it insulates plant roots, retains soil moisture, and suppresses weed growth. Our mulching service gives your landscape beds a clean, finished appearance while actively protecting your plants.

We prep the area first, removing old mulch and debris before applying a fresh, even layer that's properly graded around trees and plants.

  • Garden bed preparation and cleanup
  • Hardwood, cedar, or colored mulch options
  • Proper depth and grading around plants
  • Tree ring mulching
  • Weed barrier installation
🌳

Tree Pruning Photo

Service 03

Tree Pruning & Trimming

Healthy trees need regular pruning to remove dead or damaged growth, maintain their natural shape, and prevent hazardous branches. Our tree care service improves both the health and the look of your trees and shrubs.

Whether it's a few overgrown branches or a full shaping, we approach every tree with care and proper technique.

  • Crown thinning and shaping
  • Dead branch removal
  • Clearance pruning (structures, walkways)
  • Shrub and hedge trimming
  • Clean debris removal after service
🌾

Weed Maintenance Photo

Service 04

Weed Maintenance

Weeds compete with your lawn and garden plants for nutrients, water, and sunlight. Left unchecked, they can quickly take over. Our targeted weed maintenance keeps your property clear and lets your landscaping thrive.

We treat lawns and garden beds, targeting invasive plants at the root and applying preventative measures to slow regrowth.

  • Hand-pulling and manual removal
  • Garden bed weed clearing
  • Lawn weed treatment
  • Crack and edge weed removal
  • Preventative pre-emergent applications
